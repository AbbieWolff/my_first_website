Site Map

A description of the main pages and features of this website.

About

The About page includes a brief description of who I am and what this website is about.


Hobbies

The Video Games page includes:

  • a short blip about how and when I started playing video games,
  • what I played when I was younger, and
  • a comprehensive, somewhat orderly list of my current favorite video games, including cover art and trailers for each game.

In time, the Video Games page will be a hub for individual games or game series' pages. The trailers, cover art, and information about each game will be moved to their respective individual page.

The Books page features a short list of my favorite books with a link to my full GoodReads library.

As with the Video Games page, the Books page will become a hub for individual book or book series' pages. The cover art, descriptions, and such will be moved to the individual pages.


Art & Design

The Portfolio and Gallery pages feature my original art, photos and design projects.

The Portfolio showcases a select sampling of my work in a more formal layout.

The Gallery shows more, and more varied, pieces in a less formal layout.


Causes

The Causes page will be the home base of a variety of pages about social issues. The individual causes pages will include opportunities for learning about and improving upon the current situation.

The causes I care most about include:

  • feminism
  • body positivity
  • LGBT+ equality
  • sex positive education and culture
  • civil rights
  • environmentalism
  • poverty relief & prevention policies
  • mental health awareness

These causes matter. If you're interested, read about them, watch some videos, check out the links, spread awareness and sign petitions.


Social Media

The Social Media page(s) will offer a glimpse into the variety of activities I'm doing online at any given time: vlogging, tweeting, etc.

There will be at least one Video page connected to the Social Media page. The Video page(s) will feature original content from my YouTube channel. Many of these videos are video blogs, a.k.a. vlogs.

There will be a page showing my recent Tweets. Most of my tweets are about causes and/or games.

You can also find me on Twitter and other social media sites through links at the bottom of every page.
